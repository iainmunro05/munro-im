About Munro IM

Built by a student.
Built for students.

Munro IM was created to make construction contracts less intimidating — and more useful — for the people who work with them every day.

The story

Munro IM was built by Iain Munro, a Quantity Surveying student who noticed a gap between what university teaches and what the industry actually expects. Contract clauses are dense, technical, and often confusing — even for experienced professionals. For graduates and placement students, they can feel impenetrable.

The idea was simple: what if you could paste any clause and instantly understand what it means, what it costs, and what you should do about it? No jargon. No textbooks. Just practical, plain-English guidance built for the people on the ground.

Munro IM is that tool. It combines deep knowledge of JCT and NEC contracts with AI to give junior construction professionals the confidence to engage with contracts from day one.

What it does

Plain English

Every clause explained clearly, without unnecessary jargon.

Commercial insight

Understand the money and risk behind every clause.

QS actions

Concrete steps you should take — not vague advice.

Real examples

Practical scenarios set on UK construction projects.

Who it's for

Munro IM is designed for anyone early in their construction career — graduate QSs, assistant QSs, placement students, and junior project managers working with JCT or NEC contracts in the UK.

It is not a substitute for professional legal advice. It is an educational tool to help you understand, learn, and ask better questions. Always consult a qualified professional before acting on contract matters.
